Cake

Description

Caprese grilled cheese sandwiches are simple to make and have so much flavor! The combination of mozzarella cheese, tomatoes, and pesto gives a soft, sweet, and crunchy bite with every bite. A light and tasty lunch or dinner that is satisfying in every way!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Tomates
  • Basil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Brush the bread with olive oil
  2. Toast the bread on a pan
  3. Add the mozzarella cheese to the bread
  4. Add the tomates and basil
  5. Eat!
